BeatBox VR

BeatBox VR is a virtual reality drumming experience that lets players interact with a full drum set in a 3D environment. Inspired by the power of music and the immersive capabilities of VR, the goal was to teach fundamentals while still supporting advanced practice and performance.

Limited Space — Ludum Dare 54

A physics-driven experience focused on problem-solving and agility. Mechanics like resizing and surface manipulation encourage creative approaches to obstacles, while the first-person perspective keeps the moment-to-moment gameplay grounded and immersive.

Grey Scout — A “Sokpop” Demake

A third-person adventure blending stealth and exploration. The core loop revolves around movement, stamina management, and NPC interactions. Patrol-based enemies chase on detection, then transition into an alert state when the player successfully hides, creating a push-pull stealth rhythm.
