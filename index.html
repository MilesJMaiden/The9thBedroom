About

What is the 9TH BEDROOM?

The 9TH BEDROOM is an independent game and XR studio, built on curiosity, experimentation, and a love for immersive worlds. It’s a space for bold ideas — where boundaries between games, art, and technology are blurred.

We believe in thoughtful design, meaningful interaction, and the power of play to challenge, comfort, or transform. Every project is personal, crafted with intention and technical care.

What We Do

We design and develop interactive experiences for PC, VR, AR, and Mixed Reality.

From early prototypes to full builds, we work across Unity’s ecosystem to bring ideas to life — blending gameplay mechanics, spatial design, and emergent systems. Whether it’s an atmospheric game or an experimental XR concept, we build experiences that invite curiosity.

Why Work With Me?

As a solo developer, I offer a hands-on, detail-driven approach. You’re not dealing with layers of abstraction — just direct, focused collaboration.

With deep experience in Unity and immersive tech, I move quickly, adapt easily, and treat every project like it matters — because it does.
